About Me

Hi! My name is Caisa. I've been working on and off in customer service since I was 13 years old. I've enrolled in and dropped out of more courses than I can count because I love so many things and I want to learn forever.

What got me into web development was a detour to library sciences. In the intro course it was pretty firmly determined that library sciences were dead (I'm paraphrasing) and that the job would involve a fair amount of tech and processing data. I was like "Then why am I here? I might as well go and do that then."

So here I am. I bring with me my background in directing, editing, writing, sports coaching (!) and all my enthusiasm.
